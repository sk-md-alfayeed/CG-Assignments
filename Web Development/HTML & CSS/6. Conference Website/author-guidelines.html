Instruction to Authors

Full length manuscript/paper in ANY format should be submitted through on-line paper submission mode. Following is the link for the submission:

https://submitconnference.com/tc2021

Manuscripts for TC2021 will ONLY be accepted in electronic format through online submission system. If you are unable to submit your contribution electronically, please contact us.
